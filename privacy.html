Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental to how we build PChat. Here is exactly what we do with your data.

2. What We Absolutely Do NOT Collect

  • No plaintext passwords — all passwords are stored as secure cryptographic hashes.
  • No message content — messages are end-to-end encrypted; we cannot read them.
  • No tracking cookies — we do not use advertising or analytics tracking.
  • No device fingerprinting — we do not track your device or browser.
  • No location data — we never request or store your location.
  • No contact lists — we do not access your phone or email contacts.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create, verify, and authenticate your account securely.
  • To display your name and avatar in conversations.
  • To deliver end-to-end encrypted messages to the intended recipient.
  • To show your online status (last seen) to users you are chatting with.
  • To investigate reports and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • To send essential transactional emails (verification, password reset).

4. Data Sharing

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Legal obligations: If required by valid legal process (court order, subpoena).
  • Safety: To prevent imminent harm or illegal activity.
  • Service delivery: Only with essential service providers under strict data agreements (e.g., email delivery).

5. Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your information:

  • All connections use HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit.
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (never stored in plaintext).
  • Session tokens are cryptographically secure and expire on logout.
  • Messages are end-to-end encrypted before leaving your device.

Despite these measures, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Please use PChat responsibly and report any suspected security issues via the Report portal.

6. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Messages are stored in encrypted form to enable delivery to offline recipients. When you request account deletion, we remove your personal information within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

PChat is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has registered an account, please report it immediately through the Report portal and we will promptly remove the account.

9. Cookies & Local Storage

PChat uses:

  • Session cookies: Required for authentication — these expire when you log out.
  • Local storage: To store your session token on the device — cleared on logout.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics.

10. Third-Party Services

PChat uses UI Avatars (ui-avatars.com) to generate profile pictures from your initials. This service receives only a single letter and a color code — no personal data. No other third-party data processors are involved in the core messaging service.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated within the application. Continued use of PChat after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related requests, questions, or concerns, please use the in-app Report portal and include “Privacy Request” in the subject line. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
